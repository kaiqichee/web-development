Dino The Duck

Once upon a time there was a little duck named Dino. Dino lived with many dinosaurs and had only known dinosaurs his entire life. Dino had never seen himself (beacuse mirrors don't exist), and he grew up thinking that he was a dinosaur as well.

One days a human found the land where Dino and his dinosaur friends were living and decided to take some photos of the animals. When Dino saw the photos he was very confused because there was a duck in the photos. Dino looked around and didn't see any ducks anywhere. Then he realized that he was the duck! The end.
